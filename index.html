Hola! (Only word I know)

The main purpose of this place is to make us (I am referring to myself) — remember things, 'cause I know how we can be. We forget—sometimes a lot—and it feels like we're full of holes, like a block of cheese on some cartoon. But we created this place, to point us back, to show us home. Hence the name, Beacon.

Here live my writings, notes, fragments of memory. Pictures that caught my eye, made me feel something, or from someone I value the most. Poems I wrote, songs and playlist that I keep (most probably not mine)—all pieces I didn't want to lose.

This might be the last scrap I toss into the vast trash bin of the Internet. I promise to use every bit of energy from this world to make something that can be of use to somebody.

I promise.

"If we are lost, let this beacon take us home."

- 2023

Reminders

To me,

Hey, how have you been? If you're here to remember, take a moment and be amazed at what we've built. If you're from the future and forgot about this, hey—we made this together. Pretty cool, right? Take a seat, and warp back in time~

To the developer in me

If you are here to add or update something, please do remember to adjust the DATES! They're tiny breadcrumbs that show our journey. And please—check how it looks on different screens. A messy layout ruins the mood, you know.

To the visitor

Welcome. Truly.

You're someone I trust, or someone I wanted to share this with. You must be someone so special to me. I hope to paint even a hint of smile on that face. So... I hope you'll like it, enjoy! (I am quite short-staffed, so would you let me know if you found some typos or grammar errors? Means the world!)

“You are my lighthouse in the sea of madness.”

To the uninvited

I am not sure how did you get in here, but I would like you to leave (Like I can do much about it.). I made you a checklist to identify yourself:

  • You don't know me = uninvited
  • You know me but we are not close = uninvited (Ok but partially.)
  • Random website scrapper = uninvited
  • Hacker = Can I have your autograph? (I left you a portion where you can do E-signing, It'll be displayed randomly throughout the website. Come find it!)

I guess, you can read some of it, it's not like you can make use of them anyway. So... at least enjoy.

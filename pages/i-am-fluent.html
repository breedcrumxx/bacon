There are arguments I could have won if I had just spoken the buffers in my head. But it doesn't matter. There were conversations I could have owned, turned into something worth remembering, if I had just read the subtitles flashing in my mind. But then again, it doesn't matter. I could have carved my existence into their memory if I had just let them see the images in my head. And yet again, it doesn't matter.

A point missed, left drifting between us. And now, suddenly, it matters? Conversations that ended at hello, and suddenly they matter? Second impressions now doesn't count and opinions should be kept, then again now it matters?

But it's too late. I couldn't voice it out.

I am fluent in silence.
